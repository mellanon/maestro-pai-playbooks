### Decision Logic

```

    → Reset to Step 4, continue loop
ELIF incomplete_tasks > 0:
    → Reset to Step 4, continue loop
ELIF all_tasks_complete AND tests_pass:
    → Do NOT reset, proceed to Step 6
ELSE:
    → STOP for human review
```
